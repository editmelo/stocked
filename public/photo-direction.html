What kind of photos belong on the Stocked site — and where to find them. Five concept shots mapped to specific sections, with one-click search links into Unsplash and Pexels (filtered for real humans, no AI). Once Andre signs off on the palette, we'll match photo tones to it.

Real humans only No AI · no illustration Candid > posed Diverse representation

✓ Look for

  • Hands in action — close-ups of someone placing product on a shelf
  • Candid mid-motion shots, slight background blur
  • Real store lighting (fluorescent or warm interior, not studio)
  • Diverse representation that mirrors Andre + real retail floors
  • Natural focus and depth — not Photoshop-perfect

✗ Avoid

  • Handshake / boardroom clichés
  • Model in a clean apron grinning at the camera
  • Studio-white backgrounds
  • People holding clipboards staring at nothing
  • Anything tagged "AI generated"
